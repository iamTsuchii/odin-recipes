Classic Lasagna

A traditional lasagna, with about half the time and effort! I always cook extra to have leftovers, but the more I cook, the more they eat! Serve with garlic toast and salad.

Ingredients

  1. 1 pound ground beef
  2. 1 pound bulk Italian sausage
  3. 1/2 cup warm water
  4. cup warm water
  5. 1 pinch basil
  6. 2 teaspoons garlic powder
  7. 1 tablespoon dried oregano
  8. 1 1/2 teaspoons dried sage
  9. 2 teaspoons ground black pepper
  10. 2 tablespoons minced garlic
  11. 1 tablespoon dried onion flakes
  12. 2 (14 ounce) jars marinara sauce
  13. 1 (16 ounce) package lasagna noodles
  14. ounces ricotta cheese
  15. 1 pound mozzarella cheese, shredded
